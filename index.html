Convection Heat Transfer

Convection is the transfer of heat through fluids (gases or liquids) from a warmer spot to a cooler spot. Place an ice cube in hot coffee and the molecules on top become cooler and dense therefore moving to the bottom. If you heated the bottom of the coffee mug the opposite would happen. The more dense liquid on bottom would expand becoming lighter and would move to the top. This movement of heat is called convection.
